What is a Pallas?

A Pallas cat is a kind of feline native to Siberia, famous for its thick fur coat and ability to survive inhospitable environments.

Pallas cats need to keep good track of their daily machinations, as life in Siberia is notoriously difficult.

Like them, us humans also have to keep track of time, which is what this app is for! With this app, you can:

  • Set Daily Reminders
  • Create a Weekly Schedule
  • Set Habits to Remind Yourself of Every Day
